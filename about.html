Who We Are

A Group Built for the
Legal Ecosystem

Synaera is the holding group behind nine specialized software platforms serving the full spectrum of the legal and document management landscape. From solo notary practices to large law firms and enterprise document repositories, our portfolio delivers tailored solutions at every scale.

We are a business unit of Harris Computer, itself a subsidiary of Constellation Software Inc. (TSX: CSU) — one of the world's foremost acquirers of vertical market software businesses with over 800 companies across 40+ years of operations in 100+ countries.

🎯

Deep Domain Expertise

Decades of experience building software specifically for Quebec and Canadian legal professionals, with an intimate understanding of regulatory requirements.

🔗

Integrated Ecosystem

Our products are designed to work together — sharing data, workflows, and infrastructure across practice management, document management, and client portals.

📈

Long-Term Partnership

We don't just sell software — we commit to the long-term success of our clients with continuous product investment, training, and responsive support.

Our Mission
We buy, build, and operate vertical market software companies in legal tech, document management, and financial management — with an ownership horizon measured in decades, not quarters.

Buy

We acquire profitable, well-loved vertical software businesses with loyal user bases and strong domain fit. We move decisively, close with certainty, and hold permanently — no flipping, no fund cycles.

Build

We invest continuously in product, people, and infrastructure. Our portfolio companies benefit from shared R&D, a common compliance framework, and the operational depth of a growing group — not just capital.

Operate

We are operators first. With deep domain expertise in legal tech and document management, we coach leadership, optimize go-to-market, and unlock cross-portfolio synergies that independents simply can't access.

Constellation Software Inc. (TSX: CSU)

The World's Premier Vertical Software Acquirer

Constellation Software Inc. is one of the largest and most respected acquirers of vertical market software businesses in the world. Founded in 1995 and headquartered in Toronto, CSI has built a decentralized portfolio of hundreds of software businesses serving niche markets across six continents — with a philosophy of permanent ownership, operational autonomy, and long-term value creation.

Harris Computer is one of CSI's six operating groups, specializing in software for legal, government, healthcare, and public sector markets. Synaera operates as a business unit within Harris, focusing exclusively on legal technology and document management.
