Press Release March 2026

Harris Computer Acquires Constellio, Strengthening Its Enterprise Content Management Portfolio

Harris Computer, a subsidiary of Constellation Software Inc., has completed the acquisition of Constellio, the leading open-source enterprise search and document management platform trusted by legal, government, and financial institutions across North America and Europe.

"Constellio has built something truly exceptional — a platform that combines the power of open-source flexibility with enterprise-grade reliability. This acquisition accelerates our ability to deliver intelligent document experiences to the markets we serve."
— Harris Computer, Acquisition Statement

The acquisition marks a significant milestone for the Synaera portfolio, validating the group's investment thesis around niche vertical software with durable competitive advantages. Constellio will continue operating under its existing brand, with its Montréal-based team remaining in place.

Official Press Release — Harris Computer / Constellation Software
