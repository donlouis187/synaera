How It Works

Our Acquisition Process

A structured, respectful, and efficient process from first conversation to close.

1

Initial Conversation

A confidential, no-obligation discussion to understand your business, your goals, and whether Synaera is the right fit. We respond within 24 hours.

2

Mutual NDA & Information Exchange

We sign a mutual NDA and exchange basic business information — revenue, customer count, product overview. No lengthy questionnaires at this stage.

3

Indicative Offer

Based on initial information, we provide a non-binding indicative offer with valuation parameters within 2 weeks of receiving financials.

4

Due Diligence

Focused, efficient diligence by a team that understands legal software inside-out. We ask the right questions and move at your pace — typically 4–8 weeks.

5

Definitive Agreement

We draft and negotiate a Purchase & Sale Agreement with straightforward deal mechanics, majority cash at closing, and standard representations.

6

Close & Welcome

Transaction closes. Your team joins the Synaera family. We celebrate the milestone together and begin a structured integration plan.

7

Operational Integration

We integrate shared infrastructure and best practices while preserving what makes your product unique. Management continuity is our priority.

8

Long-Term Partnership

Permanent ownership. Continuous investment in product and people. Your business grows as part of the broader Synaera and Harris Computer ecosystem — indefinitely.
